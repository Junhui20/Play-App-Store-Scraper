### Is it worth doing? (有必要嗎?)

**Short answer: It depends on your goal.**

**YES if:**
- You want a low-effort side project generating $1K-$5K/month
- You target iOS first (zero competition)
- You differentiate with SFTP + modern UX + the feature requests above
- You treat it as a lifestyle business, not a startup

**NO if:**
- You want >$10K/month revenue
- You want high growth / VC-scale opportunity
- You only target Android (MedhaApps dominates with 5M+ downloads)

### The Smartest Play

1. **Build an iOS FTP/SFTP Server app** - 0 quality competition
2. Price at **$2.99-$4.99** (technical users pay for quality)
3. Features MedhaApps lacks: **SFTP, Tasker-like Shortcuts automation, fixed IP, idle timeout, Files app integration**
4. Then port to Android with those differentiators

### Revenue Estimate if Entering
- iOS FTP Server (quality app, $3.99): **$500-$2,000/month** within 6 months
- Android challenger with SFTP: **$300-$1,000/month** (hard to beat MedhaApps' SEO)
- Combined ceiling: **$1K-$5K/month** (realistic)
