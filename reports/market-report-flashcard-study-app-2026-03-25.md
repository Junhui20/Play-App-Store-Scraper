## 4. Recommended MVP Feature Set (Flutter Solo Developer)

### Phase 1 MVP (4-6 weeks)

1. **Flashcard engine with spaced repetition** (SRS algorithm, flip animation)
2. **Study timer** (Pomodoro-style, customizable intervals)
3. **Subject/deck organization** (folders, color coding, tags)
4. **Study statistics dashboard** (time studied, cards reviewed, streaks)
5. **Clean minimal UI** (Material 3, pastel/cute themes, dark mode from day 1)
6. **Offline-first architecture** (local SQLite/Hive, no internet required)

### Phase 2 (Weeks 7-10)

7. **Homework/assignment tracker** with due dates and reminders
8. **Grade calculator** (support multiple grading systems: US GPA, CGPA, percentage)
9. **Push notifications** (class reminders, study reminders, due dates)
10. **Import from Quizlet/Anki** (CSV import at minimum)

### Phase 3 (Weeks 11-14)

11. **Social features** (study challenges, leaderboards, friend comparison)
12. **AI flashcard generation** (paste notes/text, auto-generate Q&A cards)
13. **Calendar integration** (Google Calendar sync)
14. **Cloud sync** (Firebase, multi-device support)

### Technical Stack Recommendation (Flutter)

- **State Management:** Riverpod (recommended for solo dev, less boilerplate)
- **Local Storage:** Hive or Drift (SQLite) for offline-first
- **Backend:** Supabase (auth + database + real-time for social features)
- **AI:** OpenAI API or on-device model for flashcard generation
- **Notifications:** flutter_local_notifications + Firebase Cloud Messaging

### What Users Hate About Current Monetization

| Complaint | App | Mentions | Severity |
|---|---|---|---|
| No free trial / paywall immediately | Super Planner | 24 | CRITICAL |
| "requires subscription payment before you can even try" | Super Planner | 10 (trial) | CRITICAL |
| Free version too limited | Quizgecko | 22 | HIGH |
| "paid content is overpriced" | Lilo | 3 | MEDIUM |
| Can only add 5 grades without premium | Power Planner | 11 | HIGH |
| Paid $30 for pro and it's a waste | Super Planner | 6 | HIGH |
| "it's good but too expensive" | Quizgecko | 5 | MEDIUM |

### Recommended Monetization Model: GENEROUS FREEMIUM

**Free Tier (must be genuinely useful):**
- Unlimited flashcard decks (up to 500 cards per deck)
- Full study timer functionality
- Up to 10 subjects
- Basic statistics
- Unlimited grade entries (NOT capped at 5 like Power Planner)
- Offline mode

**Premium Tier ($2.99/month or $19.99/year):**
- AI flashcard generation (from notes, documents, images)
- Social/competitive features (leaderboards, challenges)
- Advanced analytics (study patterns, weak areas, predictions)
- Cloud sync across devices
- Custom themes
- Calendar sync
- Export to PDF

**Key Pricing Insights:**
- ALWAYS offer a 7-day free trial (Super Planner's #1 complaint is no trial)
- Price below Quizgecko (users call it "too expensive")
- Never gate core functionality behind paywall (unlike Power Planner's 5-grade limit)
- Consider a student discount or "forever free for students" marketing angle

## 9. Strategic Recommendations Summary

1. **Build a "study companion" app**, not just another flashcard app -- combine flashcards + timer + planner + grades
2. **Differentiate on reliability** -- Quizgecko's #1 issue is bugs/crashes; a stable app wins
3. **Differentiate on generosity** -- offer a truly useful free tier (Super Planner's paywall is their biggest weakness)
4. **Target Malaysian students specifically** -- SPM/STPM exam support, Bahasa Malaysia interface option, local university grading systems
5. **Lean into aesthetics** -- "cute" design drove Lilo's 94% positive rate; student audience cares about visual appeal
6. **Build social features early** -- competition/challenges are the #1 retention driver in Lilo
7. **Consider ADHD/neurodivergent angle** -- underserved, passionate user segment with no good option in MY
8. **Avoid the #1 mistake**: Never gate basic features behind a paywall without a trial period
